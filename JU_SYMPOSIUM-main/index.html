Jeppiaar University

Founded in 2022 by the visionary Dr. Jeppiaar, Jeppiaar University is a blend of academic excellence and holistic development. The campus boasts modern laboratories for every department, offering students cutting-edge facilities. Beyond academics, the university emphasizes extracurricular engagement, exemplified by its collaboration with the Computer Society of India (CSI) Club. This dynamic club hosts hackathons, hands-on sessions, and workshops, nurturing practical skills and problem-solving abilities. The synergy between Jeppiaar University and the CSI Club equips students to excel in both theoretical knowledge and real-world application, preparing them comprehensively for future challenges and opportunities.
