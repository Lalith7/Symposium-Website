About Event:

"Find the bgm."
RULES AND REGULATIONS:
1.Every student should wear an ID card.
2.Each team should have 3 members.
3.Maximum 16 teams are allowed
4.This event will have 4 rounds.
5.1st round-The game will be conducted between 2 teams where 5 bgm will be played.The one who first press the buzzer will get a chance to say answer.
6.The participants should sing the first line of the song then only the points will be given.
7.Time limit to find a song is 30 seconds.Between 2 teams who scored the highest point will go to the next round (2 nd round).These rules are same for the remaining teams.
8.2nd round -same as 1 St round.Game will be played between 2 teams who score highest points will go to 3rd round.
9.3rd round-same as before 2 rounds
10.4th round(final) -Atlast game will play between 2 teams those win in 3 rounds
11.First and second prizes will be given.
